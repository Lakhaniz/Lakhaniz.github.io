Works of Vincent Van Gogh

The Starry Night


Vincent van Gogh painted Starry night in 1889 during his stay at the asylum of Saint-Paul-de Mausole near Saint Remy de Provence. Van Gogh lived well int he hospital; he was allowed more freedoms than any of other patients. If attended, he could leave the hospital groundsl he was allowed to paint, read, and withdraw intho his own room.

Sunflowers


Van Gogh's paintings of Sunflowers are among his most famous. He did them in Arles, in the south of France, in 1888 and 1889. Vincent painted a total of five large canvases with sunflowers in a vase, with three shades of yello, and nothing else. In this way, he demonstrated that it was possible to create an image with numerous variations of a single color, without any loss of eloquence.
